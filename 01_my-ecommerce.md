##Problem statement
    -Owners want to let their pet to go outside and back in the house while stay away from home
    -Pets can be lost while they are outside the house
    -When the owners go travel or work outside for many days, they can manage the activies of the pets
##Solution(s)
    Automatic door control via Wifi and the ultrasonic collar that can detect the location of the pet
##My Ecommerce Project
    Automate pet door work with the ultasonic collar to manage pet access and detect the location while away from home
